LetThemCook! Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 February 2026

LetThemCook! respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the LetThemCook! iOS app.

This app is designed for general consumers and does not require you to create an account or sign up with an email address.

Information We Collect

LetThemCook! is built to minimise data collection. The information handled by the app is limited to what is necessary for core functionality.

Camera and Images (Menus or Your Own Photos)

  • The app allows you to capture a photo using your device camera or select an existing photo from your device to scan text (OCR).
  • Images are used temporarily on your device for cropping and text recognition (OCR).
  • Images are not stored, not uploaded, and not retained by us after processing is complete.

Text from Images (OCR Output)

  • After OCR, the recognised text may be transmitted to our servers and/or AI providers to generate recipes and related outputs.
  • We use this text only to provide the requested feature and improve app functionality.

Location Information

  • The app may request access to your precise location to identify nearby restaurants at the moment you use the feature.
  • Location data is used only in real time.
  • Location data is not stored, not linked to your identity, and not used for tracking.

Anonymous Identifier (No Sign-up Required)

  • The app may create an anonymous identifier to support core functionality such as saving app state, managing entitlements, and associating generated content with your device session.
  • This identifier is not an email address and does not require you to create an account.

Usage and Diagnostics Data

  • The app may collect anonymous usage events, such as paywall views or interactions, strictly to support app functionality and basic analytics.
  • The app may collect crash reports and performance diagnostics through Apple’s built-in tools.
  • This information does not identify you personally.

Information We Do Not Collect

LetThemCook! does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contacts
  • Account credentials (usernames/passwords)
  • Advertising identifiers for ad targeting
  • User-generated profiles
  • Stored photos or documents from your device

How We Use Information

Any information handled by the app is used only to:

  • Provide core app features, such as scanning text and (where applicable) identifying nearby restaurants
  • Generate recipes and related outputs based on recognised text
  • Display and manage subscription access
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Understand general feature usage in an anonymous way

We do not use data for advertising, marketing, or tracking across apps or websites.

Data Retention

  • Images are processed on-device and discarded immediately.
  • Location data is used only at the time of the request and is not retained.
  • Recognised text and generated outputs may be retained as needed to provide features such as recipe history (if enabled in the app) and to support app operation.
  • Anonymous usage and diagnostic data may be retained in aggregated form to support app operation and improvements.

Third-Party Services

LetThemCook! uses the following third-party services solely to operate the app:

Apple Services

  • StoreKit for In-App Purchases and subscriptions. Payment information is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store payment details.
  • Apple’s crash and performance diagnostics tools.

Superwall

  • Superwall is used to display paywalls and help manage subscription entitlements.
  • Superwall may collect device information and usage events (for example, paywall views or interactions) to provide analytics and improve performance.

No third-party services are used for advertising or cross-app tracking.

User Privacy Rights

You control access to device features such as the camera, photo library, and location through iOS system settings.

You can:

  • Deny or revoke camera, photo library, or location permissions at any time
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID settings

Because the app does not require sign-up with personal details, there is no personal user profile to access, modify, or delete.

Children’s Privacy

LetThemCook! is not directed to children under the age of 13.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us so we can address the issue.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
